About Neymar Júnior

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior (Brazilian Portuguese pronunciation: [nejˈmaʁ dɐ ˈsiwvɐ ˈsɐ̃tuz ˈʒũɲoʁ] ⓘ; born 5 February 1992), also known as Neymar Júnior or simply Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a left winger, attacking midfielder or forward for Campeonato Brasileiro Série A team Santos, whom he captains, and the Brazil national team. Often praised as one of the best players of his era, he is known for his flair, dribbling, playmaking, and finishing. He is Brazil's all-time top goalscorer.

Achievements

->He is the nation's all-time top goalscorer, with 79 goals in 128 matches. Neymar has won the Globe Soccer Player Career Award and being named Brazil's all-time top goal scorer. Neymar is mostly famous for his dazzling dribbling abilities, goal-scoring prowess and playmaking skills. Neymar's favourite skill is step-over cut.
